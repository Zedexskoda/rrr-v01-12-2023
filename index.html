At Revive Retro Ride, we believe that the golden eras of automobiles deserve a place in the present. We're passionate about reviving the timeless charm of classic and vintage vehicles, and sharing that experience with fellow enthusiasts.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

  • What is Paint Protection Film (PPF) and how does it work?

    Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a clear, durable film that is applied to the exterior of a vehicle to protect it from stone chips, scratches, bug splatter, and other environmental damage. It acts as a barrier between the vehicle's paint and the elements.

  • How long does PPF last on a vehicle?

    With proper care, PPF can last up to 5-10 years. However, factors like sun exposure, driving conditions, and maintenance practices can affect its longevity.

  • What are the benefits of Ceramic Coating?

    Ceramic Coating is a liquid polymer that is applied to the vehicle's exterior. It forms a protective layer that enhances gloss, provides hydrophobic properties, and offers strong chemical resistance. It also makes the car easier to clean.

  • Can PPF and Ceramic Coating be applied together?

    Yes, PPF and Ceramic Coating can be applied together for enhanced protection. PPF provides a physical barrier against scratches and stone chips, while Ceramic Coating adds an extra layer of chemical resistance and gloss.

  • How long does it take to apply PPF and Ceramic Coating?

    The time required for application depends on the size and complexity of the vehicle. Typically, PPF installation takes 1-2 days, while Ceramic Coating may require an additional 1-2 days for proper curing.

  • Is Ceramic Coating a substitute for waxing?

    Yes, Ceramic Coating can be considered a long-lasting alternative to traditional waxing. It provides superior protection and requires less frequent reapplication.
